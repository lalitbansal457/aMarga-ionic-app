Some selected opinions

It gladdens me to learn that Guru Shrii A’nandamu’rtijii (Prabhat Ranjan Sarkar) has composed these versatile songs full of meaning and lyrical beauty in mere eight years. I only wish had I taken birth 25 years later, I would have had the opportunity to sing these songs.
-Padma Vibhushan Pandit Jas Raj (Eminent Classical Singer)

Sarkar’s visionary philosophy, ideals and actional plans clearly point that His is a renaissance movement, the need of the present day’s troubled humanity. Such a movement will surely spread and the humanity will accept it.
-Dom Moraes (Noted Poet & Journalist)

Everybody should be benefited by increase in production. How it can happen-has been explained by Shrii P.R. Sarkar in his PROUT theory. Decentralization, not centralization of economic power is the way out.
-Prof. Debnarayan Sarkar (Secy. Center for economic studies, Presidency University)

These beautiful Prabhat samgiit songs composed and set to tunes by Shrii Sarkar need to be brought to the world’s attention at the earliest.
-Padma Vibhushan Ustad Amjad Ali Khan (Eminent Sarod Player)

The songs composed by Him, the vast amount of literature given by Him stand out as the brightest jewel of His noble personality. I extend all my good will and cooperation in propagating His teachings.
-Vijay Tendulkar (Eminent Marathi Play Writer)

In general we find Sarkar’s works exemplary for the following reasons. In terms of economic his work is strong on both growth and distribution dimensions. Sarkar is also eclectic in his theory of political economy drawing on market and regulatory mechanisms. Alienation is a result not of private property but of the concentration of wealth and of the location of the self in a materialistic paradigm…
-Prof. Sohail Inayatullah(Member of World future Studies Federation & Prof. Queensland University, Australia)

It is treat to hear the Prabhat Samgiit songs composed by Shrii Sarkar. They are fresh in style and rhythm. The world will surely know about them soon.
-Padma Vibhushan Pandit Shiv Kumar Sharma (Eminent Santoor Player)

I am delighted to hear the Prabhat samgiit songs composed by Shrii Prabhat Ranjan Sarkar. They are exceptionally good in meter and lyrical beauty. I and my Institute os Hindusthani Music will always be ready to extend any help in propagating these beautiful compositions.
Naushad (Famous Music Director)

I won’t forget this evening of RAWA. I generally communicate with the people through colours and canvas. Shri Sarkar delivered an insight into the devotional characteristics and a new impact of Prabhat Samgiita in this age of sheer intensity and gruesome speed.
-Shrii Ramananda Bandopadhyay (Eminent painter and artist in sculpture)

The writings of Shrii P.R. Sarkar are refreshing and informative on all subjects that concern human life. As a writer I wish to pay my respect to Him for His profound knowledge.
-Shankar (Shrii Mani Shankar Mukherjee)(Eminent Bengali Novelist & writer)
